Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on StudentHub?

Click on the Register page, complete the registration form, and submit it.

How do I log in?

Use your registered email address and password on the Login page.

How can I update my profile?

Open the Profile page and edit your personal information.

Where can I view upcoming events?

Visit the Events page to see all upcoming workshops, seminars, and campus activities.

How do I contact StudentHub support?

Visit the Contact page and fill out the contact form or use the provided email and phone number.
